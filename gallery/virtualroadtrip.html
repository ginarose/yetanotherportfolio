Virtual Roadtrip

About:

Using the power of Google and Flickr, I brought to life a conceptual group project from my last semester of Digital Media studies at the University of Central Florida. I have facilitated an interactive "roadtrip" by allowing the user to explore the map and showing them Flickr uploads tagged with the geolocation clicked.

Tool(s) used:

HTML/CSS, jQuery, Google Maps API, Flickr API.
