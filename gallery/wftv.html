9 Family Connection Internship

About:

As part of my internship with WFTV's 9 Family Connection department, I had an ongoing role in producing, filming and editing videos for their website. Many included filming on my own, with only basic direction. I was able to produce the weekly 9 Family Minute , as well as cover special events like Paws in the Park and this year's Polar Plunge.

Tool(s) used:

Canon digital SLR cameras, Panasonic digital camcorders, Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Encore
