Dredbeard Landing Page designs

About:

The University of Central Florida Center for Distributed Learning recently underwent a internal software overhaul. Our timelogging and request ticketing system, the Dredbeard suite, was massively updated. I was in charge of coming up with ideas for a new landing page that links all the tools for internal use. I brainstormed closely with a coworker, Elise Heron, to come up with usable and creative ideas. To see other options we came up with, view this PDF .

Tool(s) used:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, pen & paper.
