Earth Day mixed media

About:

For earth day, I wanted to do a play on words with the traditional saying of "Nature vs. Nurture," and came up with this. It's a bit of a mixed media project in that I digitally and traditionally lettered words, used some typography and combined it with my photography. I enjoy doing quick daily exercises with handlettering and using art as a vehicle for words that I find endearing or inspirational.

Tool(s) used:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Canon 60D, acrylic paint, and a font called Salaryman
