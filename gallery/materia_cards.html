Materia playing cards

About:

As a hack day project, I took a card back design I previously came up with for a Materia widget I worked on and pushed it to the next level. For one day, I worked on developing 'Kogneato' (Materia's iconic robot) into various royal cards and of course, the joker. From there, a coworker had the cards printed and the idea had come to reality!

Tool(s) used:

Adobe Illustrator
